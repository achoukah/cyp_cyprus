Biography

CYP CYPRUS started writing music back in 1993 as a way to escape the chaos of a south east London council estate.

Coming from a broken home and environment where everything is designed to hold you down, Hip-hop gave a young Cyp power and a voice to express himself.

He quickly established a local name for himself as a raw lyricist but never really branched out or took his art to the next level until now.

Many things have happened in life that will help us to grow and now THE ACTIVIST is ready and dedicated towards his message for all people in the struggle in hope he can change and help them to change for the better.
